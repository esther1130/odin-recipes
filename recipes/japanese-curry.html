Japanese Curry Recipe

Japenese curry is the classic comfort food that is quick and easy to make.

This dish commonly includes carrots, potatoes and onions, but you can customise to your liking. (I like to add tomatoes and green peas.)

Ingredients:

  • Japanese curry cubes

    (you can use Vermont or Golden brand.)

  • Half an onion, cut into cubes
  • one carrot, cut into cubes
  • two potatoes, cut into cubes

Steps:

  1. add the curry cubes to 3 cubes of boiled water.
  2. Add all the vegetables once the cubes and fully dissolved
  3. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes until the carrots and potatoes and soft
  4. serve with rice
